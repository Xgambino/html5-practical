Steps to Buy a Property

  1. Browse available listings.
  2. Schedule a viewing with our agents.
  3. Negotiate and agree on terms.
  4. Secure financing or mortgage approval.
  5. Finalize purchase agreement and ownership transfer.

Real Estate Glossary

Mortgage
A loan secured by property, usually to buy a home.
Escrow
A financial arrangement where a neutral third party holds funds until conditions are met.
Appraisal
An estimate of a property’s value, typically conducted before a sale or financing.
ROI
ROI is the expected profitability from property investments.
